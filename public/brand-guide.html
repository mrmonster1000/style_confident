Using in Canva

Quick Setup in Canva

All three fonts (Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond, Libre Franklin) are available in Canva. To set up your brand kit:

  • Go to Brand Kit in Canva (requires Canva Pro)
  • Add the hex codes above as your brand colours
  • Set Playfair Display as your headline font
  • Set Cormorant Garamond as your body font
  • Set Libre Franklin as your accent/label font
